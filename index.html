Datasheet for the Project "Smart Citizen Kit / Platform"

Essential project information including title, authors, timeline, format, and target audience

Title: Smart Citizen Kit & Platform
Author(s): Fab Lab Barcelona, Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia (IAAC), various contributors (e.g. Guillem Camprodon, Óscar González, Víctor Barberán, Máximo Pérez, etc.)
Year: Project inception in 2012, key hardware publication in 2019, enclosures documentation updated through 2023
Format: Open‑source hardware (Smart Citizen Kits, Stations), documentation website, datasets, academic article (HardwareX), firmware and platform software
Audience: Citizen‑science participants, researchers, educators, environmental activists, community groups, open hardware developers, policymakers
