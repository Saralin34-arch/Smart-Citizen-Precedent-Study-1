Smart Citizen

open-source tools for collective environmental action

OVERVIEW

FAB LAB BARCELONA

The Smart Citizen Documentation website is a comprehensive open-source knowledge hub for the Smart Citizen Kit and related sensing projects. Created by Fab Lab Barcelona (IAAC) and maintained under free licenses, it provides detailed guidance on everything from hardware design and firmware architecture to installation, data management, and community-driven deployment strategies. The platform promotes citizen science by equipping individuals and communities with low-cost, modular environmental sensors—measuring air quality, noise, light, and more—and supporting public engagement through downloadable resources, API access, and step-by-step guides. It emphasizes open, participatory methodologies and offers practical tools for education, research, and local action, all rooted in a commitment to transparency, reuse, and co-created environmental monitoring.
