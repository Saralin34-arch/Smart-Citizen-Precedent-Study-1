OVERVIEW

FAB LAB BARCELONA

Smart Citizen is a collaborative platform maintained by Fab Lab Barcelona since 2012. It enables citizens and researchers to monitor and share environmental data using open-source hardware and software.

  • Licenses: CERN OHL v1.2, GNU GPL v3.0, GNU AGLP v3.0
  • Data: Stored via SmartCitizen Platform, Micro-SD, and SQL
  • Audience: Citizens, Scientists, Designers, Urban Thinkers

DESIGN CONCEPT

CITIZEN MONITORING SCIENCES

Rooted in citizen monitoring sciences, Smart Citizen promotes open participation in environmental research and advocacy. The platform is guided by principles of co-creation, change-making, empowerment, and openness.

  • Monitoring of air, water, and biodiversity
  • Community-based action frameworks
  • Control over data access and privacy

TECHNOLOGY

SMART CITIZEN KIT

  • Smart Citizen Kit: Pre-assembled, plug-and-play device for air quality sensing
  • Connectivity: MQTT and HTTP support for real-time data flow
  • Setup: Domestic balconies with stable Wi-Fi and power access
  • API: developer.smartcitizen.me
  • Docs: Data Platform Documentation
