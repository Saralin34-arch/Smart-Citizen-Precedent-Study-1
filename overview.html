OVERVIEW

FAB LAB BARCELONA

Smart Citizen is a collaborative platform maintained by Fab Lab Barcelona since 2012. It enables citizens and researchers to monitor and share environmental data using open-source hardware and software.

  • Licenses: CERN OHL v1.2, GNU GPL v3.0, GNU AGLP v3.0
  • Data: Stored via SmartCitizen Platform, Micro-SD, and SQL
  • Audience: Citizens, Scientists, Designers, Urban Thinkers
